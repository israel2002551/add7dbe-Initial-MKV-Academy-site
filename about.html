About MKV Academy

The educational gateway into a larger engineering ecosystem — built on real consulting work, not generic tutorials.

Born from a consulting practice, not a course catalog

MKV Academy is an initiative born from MKV Consulting, a company co-founded by twin brothers Joseph-Celestine Donald, a Marine Engineer, and Diamond, an Electrical Engineer. Together, they believe engineering education, innovation, and practical technical skills have the power to transform industries and create opportunities throughout Africa and beyond.

Their mission is to build an ecosystem where aspiring engineers, professionals, hardware startups, manufacturers, and engineering teams gain access to practical education, technical consulting, mentorship, and industry-ready skills.

At the center of this vision is Joseph-Celestine Donald, recognized as one of the youngest engineering experts in his field. His journey has been defined by continuous learning, solving real engineering problems, mentoring engineers, leading technical communities, building engineering partnerships, teaching CAD and simulation software, and helping companies improve their engineering workflows.

What we believe

  • Practical skill beats theoretical knowledge when it's time to ship a real part.
  • Engineering opportunity should not be limited by geography — Africa's engineers deserve the same access as anywhere else.
  • Mentorship, not just content, is what turns a student into a working engineer.
