About MKV Academy

The educational gateway into a larger engineering ecosystem

MKV Academy is an initiative born from MKV Consulting, a company co-founded by twin brothers Joseph-Celestine Donald, a Marine Engineer, and Diamond, an Electrical Engineer. Together, they believe engineering education, innovation, and practical technical skills have the power to transform industries and create opportunities throughout Africa and beyond.

We're building an ecosystem where aspiring engineers, professionals, hardware startups, manufacturers, and engineering teams gain access to practical education, technical consulting, mentorship, and industry-ready skills — not just another course platform.
